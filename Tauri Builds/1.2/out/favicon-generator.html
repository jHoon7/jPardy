Favicon Generator

This page displays the "!" character in the Gyparody font.

To create your favicon:

  1. Take a screenshot of the blue square with the "!" character
  2. Open the screenshot in an image editor
  3. Crop it to just the blue square
  4. Save it as "favicon.ico" in the public folder

Alternatively, you can use a browser extension or online tool to convert this page to a favicon.
